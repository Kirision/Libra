about the event

"Book Club" gathering

It brings together a group of individuals who have read a particular book and are interested in discussing it. The event is usually scheduled on a specific day every month and open to anyone who wants to join. During the Book Club event, participants engage in a meaningful conversation about the selected book.

this brings us closer

This event not only promotes a love for reading but also fosters a sense of community among individuals who share a passion for literature. It provides a platform for readers to connect, exchange ideas, and explore different perspectives. Overall, a Book Club event in a library is a great way to discover new books, expand one's literary horizons, and engage in lively discussions with fellow book enthusiasts.
